Lemonade Stand

Hi! Welcome to Lemonsville, California!

In this small town, you are in charge of running your own lemonade stand. How much profit you make is up to you.

To manage your lemonade stand you will need to make these decisions every day:

  1. How many glasses of lemonade to make (only one batch is made each morning)
  2. How many advertising signs to make (this signs cost fifteen cents each)
  3. What price to charge for each glass

You will begin with $2.00 cash (assets). Because your mother gave you some sugar, your cost to make lemonade is two cents a glass (this may change in the future.
